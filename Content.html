How to Rent a Dragon
Open the DragonBarn app on your phone. Once you have gotten past our loading page, simply click on the button that is labeled with "Rent a Dragon". A map will pop up, and show you which dragon is coming and where they are. However, if you have a party of three or more, please press the "Party Bus" option before you rent your next ride. Billing will be charged from our account after you're at your destination.

Behind the Scenes
If you've ever wondered what goes on behind the scenes at DragonBarn, you've come to the right place! Once a customer calls a dragon, the dragon will be groomed, fed, given water, saddled, and sent on its way. (Dragons have a wonderful sense of direction.) Once the dragon has fulfilled its duty, it returns back to our stables to a welcoming feast of sheep, which we raise on site. After drinking the water it needs, the dragon is free to play with its stablemates, sleep, or chase around the terrorized sheep at out farms. Our hatcheries are very well controlled, and after a dragon hatches, it will be trained diligently until it, too, is ready to fly. Then, flying lessons will be arranged. Once the dragon is completely ready, it is sent on its way to our stables. After a dragon has had at least 25 years of service with us, or is 50 years old, it will be retired to a pasture of sheep, a lake, and whatever it needs.
